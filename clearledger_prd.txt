# ClearLedger: Blockchain Public Fund Tracking Dashboard (India)

## Overview
A React-based government dashboard for tracking public funds using blockchain technology.

## Core Components
### Sidebar
- Navigation: Dashboard, Fund Ledger, Village Map, Flag & Report, Verify.
- Icons: Serious, minimal SVG icons.
- Branding: "ClearLedger" logo.
- Footer: "MetaMask Connected: Sepolia Testnet" with a green status dot.

### Topbar
- Dynamic page title.
- Pulsing green dot (live blockchain connection).
- Role switcher: Public / Auditor / Admin.

## Pages
### Dashboard
- Metrics (Glassmorphism cards):
    - Total Allocated: ₹86,000 Cr
    - Reached Ground: ₹71,240 Cr
    - Flagged Leakage: ₹169.75 Cr
    - Active AI Alerts: (Live counter)
- Charts: 
    - Line chart: Allocated vs Received (12 months).
    - Bar chart: State-wise misappropriation (Indian states).
- AI Anomaly Alerts panel: Recent suspicious activities with status dots (Red/Amber).

### Fund Ledger
- Data-dense table: From, To (Village), Amount (₹), Tx Hash (0x...), Timestamp, Status (Verified/Flagged/Pending).
- Interactions: Expandable rows showing "AI suspicion score" (e.g., 85/100) and "IPFS proof hash."

### Village Map
- Layout: Full-width map of the Republic of India.
- Data: Granular markers for Gram Panchayats (villages).
- Clustering: State/District clusters zooming into exact locations.
- Legend: Red (Flagged), Amber (Pending), Green (Verified).
- Side Panel: Map stats (Total Villages Tracked, High-Risk zones).

### Flag & Report
- Form: Scheme (MGNREGA, PM Awaas, etc.), State, District, Amount, Description.
- Action: "Analyse with AI + Record on Blockchain" button (Glowing blue).
- Result: "AI Analysis Result" panel (Risk score, next steps).

### Verify (Citizen Search)
- Search: Large center input "Enter your village or Gram Panchayat name to verify funds."
- Result Card: Allocated vs Received on-chain, "Blockchain Confirmed" green badge.
